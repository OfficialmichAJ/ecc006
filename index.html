HW 2

  1. How can you join WebProfessionals.org? What does it cost to join?
    Answer:

    If you want to become a member of WebProfessionals.org, you can go to their website at www.webprofessionals.org and find the "Join Us" button on the main page. This will direct you to the membership page where you can choose the kind of membership you want to apply for.
    1- Individual Membership: This is for people who work in the web industry as designers, developers, project managers, marketers, and other related fields. The price of individual membership is $99 per year.
    2- Student Membership: This is for students who are studying in a degree or certificate program related to web design, development, or a related field. The price of student membership is $49 per year.
    3- Corporate Membership: This is for businesses or organizations that want to give their employees access to the resources and benefits offered by WebProfessionals.org. The price of corporate membership depends on the number of employees and other factors.
    To apply for membership, you will need to give some basic information about yourself or your business, as well as payment information if you are applying for individual or student membership. After your application is checked and approved, you will get access to the member benefits and resources offered by WebProfessionals.org
